20/20 - it speaks of perfect vision, of unclouded sight and a clear picture. As we enter 2020 I want us to make sure we have a clear vision moving forward. I wanted to share some thoughts that I hope will help guide our thoughts as we look forward to the new opportunities this brings.

For as 2 Corinthians 3 says:

>But we all, with unveiled face, beholding as in a mirror the glory of the Lord, are being transformed into the same image from glory to glory, just as from the Lord, the Spirit.

So let's make 2020 the year that we have a clear view of Jesus, a view clear from sin
